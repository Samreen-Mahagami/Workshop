Medical Transcription: It identifies complex drug names (like Metformin or Zyrtec) and conditions (like Hypertension).

Speaker Partitioning: It knows exactly when the Doctor is speaking versus the Patient.

Clinical Summarization: This is the "magic." It analyzes the conversation and organizes it into a SOAP Note (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan), which is the global standard for medical record-keeping.
